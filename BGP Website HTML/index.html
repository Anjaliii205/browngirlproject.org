What It Means to Be a Woman of Color in the U.S.

Our first research report draws on findings from a two-phase qualitative study — 22 participants across three focus groups and individual interviews. Real voices. Rigorous research. Given back to the community it came from.

About

Founded in 2022 by Anjali Tallapragada, Brown Girl Project began as graduate thesis research at NYU and has grown into an independent research and community practice. Our work sits at the intersection of qualitative research, community engagement, and knowledge return.

We believe research belongs to the communities it studies — not just to institutions.
